About Me

My name is Dylan.

I was born in Prince Edward Island, Canada and moved to Nova Scotia in 2012 to study and pursue a career in photography. After freelancing for a few years I decided to switch up my career and enrolled in the web programming stream at NSCC.

I quickly realized that I should have picked up programming earlier as I've always had a strong passion for problem solving, and I enjoy creating tools to help people. I love that the web world is constantly changing and evolving. There's always somethign new to learn.
